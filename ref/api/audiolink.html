AudioLink – A link to an audio recording¶

class typepad.api.AudioLink(**kwargs)¶

A link to an audio recording.

duration¶

The duration of the audio stream in seconds.

This property will be omitted if the length of the audio stream could not be determined.

url¶
The URL of an MP3 representation of the audio stream.
